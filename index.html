My Daughters Favorite Animals

Isabel's Site

My daughter loves animals so I thought my first website would a website for her. I am going to show three of her favorites and some fun facts about them so she can mess around on this website.

Owls

  • Owls have far-sighted, tubular eyes
  • Owls have super-powered hearing
  • Owl flight is silent

Bush Baby

  • Bush babies are nocturnal
  • They can not move their eyes
  • Their ears protect them while jumping

Dogs

  • Their sense of smell is at least 40x better than ours
  • Dogs can sniff at the same time as breathing
  • Dogs don't sweat like we do
